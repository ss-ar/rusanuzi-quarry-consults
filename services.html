Our Services

Our services span from large-scale civil engineering projects to specialized mining operations, with a strong focus on delivering results that not only meet but exceed industry standards.

Civil Works

Rusanuzi Quarry Consult offers a broad range of civil engineering services, from the planning and construction of infrastructure projects to site development and road construction. Our team of experienced engineers and construction professionals ensures that every project is executed with precision, on time, and within budget.

Key Services
  1. Site Preparation. Clearing, grading, and levelling of construction sites.
  2. Road Construction. Design and construction of roads, highways, and bridges
  3. Infrastructure Development. Construction of commercial, industrial, and residential structures.
  4. Drainage Systems. Design and installation of effective drainage systems to manage water flow and prevent flooding.
  5. Earthworks. Excavation, trenching, and land reclamation.

Mining Consultancy

Our mining services cover the entire mining lifecycle, from exploration and extraction to processing and rehabilitation. We provide expert consultancy services that help clients navigate the complexities of mining operations, ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements and optimising operational efficiency.

Key Services
  1. Exploration. Geological surveys, resource estimation, and feasibility studies.
  2. Extraction. Efficient and safe extraction of minerals using advanced mining techniques.
  3. Processing. Crushing, milling, and processing of minerals to meet client specifications.
  4. Environmental Management. Implementation of sustainable practices to minimize environmental impact.
  5. Regulatory Compliance. Assistance with obtaining permits and ensuring compliance with local and international mining regulations.

Quarrying Consultancy

Our quarrying consultancy services are designed to help clients optimise their quarry operations. We provide expert advice on everything from site selection and resource estimation to operational planning and execution, and environmental management.

Key Services
  1. Site Selection. Identifying and evaluating potential quarry sites.
  2. Resource Estimation. Assessing the quantity and quality of resources available.
  3. Operational Planning. Developing efficient, cost-effective quarrying plans.
  4. Environmental Management. Implementing best practices to minimise environmental impact.
  5. Health and Safety Compliance. : Ensuring that all quarrying operations comply with health and safety regulations.

Rock Drilling and Blasting

Rusanuzi Quarry Consult is a leading provider of rock drilling and blasting services, catering to the construction and mining industries. We employ advanced drilling techniques and controlled blasting methods to achieve precise results while maintaining the highest safety standards.

Key Services
  1. Drilling. Precision drilling for construction, quarrying, and mining projects.
  2. Blasting. Controlled blasting to break down rocks for easy excavation and processing.
  3. Vibration Monitoring. Monitoring and controlling vibrations to ensure safety and minimise impact on surrounding structures.
  4. Environmental Management. Implementing measures to reduce dust, noise, and other environmental impacts.
  5. Safety Protocols. Strict adherence to safety standards to protect workers and the public.
