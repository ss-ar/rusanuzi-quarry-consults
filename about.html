Who
are we?

Rusanuzi Quarry Consult is a premier service provider in the civil works, mining, and quarrying industries, offering comprehensive solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients. Founded with a vision to drive innovation and excellence, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner in the country, known for our commitment to quality, safety, and sustainability.

Our services span from large-scale civil engineering projects to specialised mining operations, with a strong focus on delivering results that not only meet but exceed industry standards. With a team of seasoned professionals and a fleet of state-of-the-art equipment, we are equipped to handle projects of any scale and complexity. Rusanuzi Quarry Consult as a company was established in 2015 and is fully registered in Uganda (TIN: 1008348301).
